Platform Engineer / Cloud Architect / AI Engineer

I'm Yassin, a platform engineer and cloud architect with over six years of experience in IT and Cloud Engineering, and the past three years focused on building production grade cloud platforms, data systems, and applied AI products. I began in hands on IT support and infrastructure operations, then progressively specialised in Azure and AWS, Kubernetes platform engineering, infrastructure as code, governance, MLOps, and API driven systems. I hold the Azure Solutions Architect Expert certification, Azure Administrator Associate, Certified Kubernetes Administrator, multiple Microsoft 365 certifications, and CompTIA Network+. Today I design and build end to end systems spanning infrastructure, APIs, data pipelines, analytics, compliance, and applied AI. A core principle in my work is deterministic first system design, where systems are correct, traceable, and governed by default, and AI is layered as an assistive capability rather than a decision engine. This approach underpins projects such as my compliance engine built from legislation.gov.uk data, analytics and ML reporting pipelines, location intelligence platforms, AWS MLOps deployments, and asset management systems. Alongside this profile, I include live demo videos showcasing production systems I've built, including an AWS MLOps platform, executive analytics and ML reporting pipelines, a UK legislation driven compliance engine, an asset management platform, and a location intelligence product. These demonstrate not only technical depth, but my ability to design complete, deployable systems and turn complex requirements into working products. I'm highly self driven, learn quickly, and enjoy operating across domains, from infrastructure and governance to data, ML, and product engineering. My focus is always on building systems that are reliable in production, understandable by humans, and viable commercially.

Compliance Engine (Legislation to Cloud Constraints)
